Mike Goldin

12/06/2018 - Parker

  1. Who are you and what are you working on?
  1. Software developer at Consensys
  2. Known for Token Curated Registries 1.0. Works on team turning TCR’s into product which in turn informs the engineering/design. We fully intend the projects to succeed so we can incorporate feedback. It’s all still experimental, though.
  3. TCR came from AdChain. Deployed to Ethereum mainnet on April 24th. Useful for data on how people interact when there’s actual money at stake. New type of TCR where token supply inflates after every vote.
  4. Adchain allows publishers and suppliers to find each other on a p2p.
  5. By using TCRs to curate who may participate, cut out middlemen as well as bad actors.
  6. Focus is on onboarding.

  3. Made technical assumptions early on, problem could only be discovered when going into production. User data you get from running on a testnet. People’s behavior changes as you add real money.
